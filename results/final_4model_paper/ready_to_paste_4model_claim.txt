Main 4-model result claim:

Across three biomedical QA datasets, MedQA, MedMCQA, and PubMedQA, with 1,000 examples per dataset, the proposed answer-fusion plus HypothesisMed-v3 SPACE pipeline improves weighted answer accuracy over the best Direct/CoT baseline for every evaluated model.

- Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct: proposed accuracy 0.6367 vs 0.6003 for Chain-of-thought; absolute gain 0.0364, relative gain 6.06%; SPACE coverage 0.9997; false commitment 0.1026.
- Phi-4-mini-instruct: proposed accuracy 0.5767 vs 0.4817 for Chain-of-thought; absolute gain 0.0950, relative gain 19.72%; SPACE coverage 0.9920; false commitment 0.4054.
- DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Qwen-32B: proposed accuracy 0.3877 vs 0.1690 for Direct; absolute gain 0.2187, relative gain 129.41%; SPACE coverage 0.4430; false commitment 0.0213.
- biomistral_biomistral_7b: proposed accuracy 0.1307 vs 0.1247 for Chain-of-thought; absolute gain 0.0060, relative gain 4.81%; SPACE coverage 0.0040; false commitment 0.0000.

Recommended interpretation:
The proposed method should be framed as an inference-time reliability pipeline. Majority answer fusion improves answer accuracy, while HypothesisMed-v3 supplies structured SPACE labels and confidence information for reliability analysis.

Caveat:
DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Qwen-32B has low Direct/CoT parse coverage in this setup, so DeepSeek should be described as a formatting-stress-test model rather than a clean capability comparison.
